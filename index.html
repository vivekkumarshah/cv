Shree lodge and activities

Information

Tucked away in Dandeli, haven for nature buffs, Shree Lodge offers guests the opportunity to enjoy a pleasant stay with a homely atmosphere. It is an excellent retreat from city life, and meets all the requirements of leisure travellers with aplomb. Surrounded by dense forests, Dandeli is bestowed with a plethora of unexplored and uncharted spots. Set amid tranquil environs and lush greenery, the hotel features comfortable rooms that are equipped with requisite amenities. All bedrooms are flooded with natural light, ideal for family vacations amidst the splendorous bounties of nature. The property can be reached from Hubli Airport and Alnavar Junction. Have a wonderful stay at Shree Lodge.
